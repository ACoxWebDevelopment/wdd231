club meeting times

The club meets every 2nd Tuesday at the home/workshop of Kris Kringle. He generously allows us to store the club cutting and polishing equipment in his workshop

dues

Dues are $20/yr per indiviual or $50/yr for a family membership. Membership includes a subscription to our monthly newsletter, access to club equipment (contact Kris Kringle 406-555-1234) to arrange, and a 20% discount on tickets to the Three Forks Gem and Mineral show.

general information

Please see our gallery for common minerals found in Montana. The list is by no means complete. If you can't identify a rock, just ask, we'll be happy to help. You can find semi-precious stones pretty much anywhwere in Montana. See our useful links for some ideas to get started.
